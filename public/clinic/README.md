## Tips
- **Landscape orientation** works best (roughly 3:2 or 4:3). Portrait photos get
  cropped to fill.
- Keep them reasonably sized (long edge ~1600px, < ~500 KB) so pages stay fast.
- JPG or WebP are both fine — if you use WebP, rename to `*.jpg` **or** ask me to
  switch the paths.

To wire a photo into more places (e.g. a real doctor portrait to replace the
placeholder in `components/Doctor.tsx`), just add the file and let me know.
